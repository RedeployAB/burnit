Information that is collected

This application does not collect or store any personal information, except for the following:

  • Source IP Address:
    • Logging: To maintain a record of access for operational security.
    • Rate limiting: To prevent abuse and ensure fair access.

No additional personal data, cookies or trackers are used.

How the information is used

The information collected is used strictly for:

  1. Maintaining service security: Protecting the application from misuse.
  2. Operations: Assisting with eventual troubleshooting.
  3. Rate limiting: Ensuring access to the application without interference from excessive or malicious use.

Data retention

IP address logs are only stored for the time necessary to achieve the purposes listed above.

Data sharing

The application is hosted on a third-party service provider. As part of this, logs containing source IP addresses may be stored on their infrastructure. These logs are stored only for the purposes listed above.
