Information that is collected

This application does not collect or store any personal information, except for the following:

  • Source IP Address:
    • Logging: To maintain a record of access for operational security.
    • Rate limiting: To prevent abuse and ensure fair access.

No additional personal data, cookies or trackers are used. Exceptions to this might be due to third-party service providers, such as hosting providers. See the section below for more information on data sharing.

How the information is used

The information collected by the application is for the purposes of:

  1. Maintaining service security: Protecting the application from misuse.
  2. Operations: Assisting with eventual troubleshooting.
  3. Rate limiting: Ensuring access to the application without interference from excessive or malicious use.

Data retention

In the context of the application, IP address logs are only stored for the time necessary to achieve the purposes listed above.

In the case of the application being hosted on a third-party service provider, the retention period may vary. See the section below for more information on data sharing.

Data sharing

The application may be hosted on the infrastructure of a third-party service provider. When hosted on such infrastructure, logs containing source IP addresses may be stored there. Please note that the specific hosting provider and their data handling practices may vary.
